# how to create a course

## step 1 - plan your course 
- plan your course using `plan-course` flow 
- check if you like the content and itterate until you do  

## step 2 - plan your first module  
- plan your course using `plan-module` flow 
- check if you like the content and itterate until you do  

## step 3 - plan your first module  
- write your chapters for first module `write-chapters` flow 
- check if you like the content and itterate until you do
